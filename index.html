SmartGas: AI-Powered Gas Cylinder Monitoring System

Lead: Odusanya Ibukun Adewale

Role: Lead Cloud Engineer | AI Enthusiast

Welcome to my innovative project—an AI-powered gas cylinder monitoring system designed to tackle a common yet critical challenge faced by many households and businesses across Africa.

Our solution introduces smart, real-time monitoring powered by AI and IoT sensors. Users receive timely alerts when gas levels are low, enabling them to plan ahead and avoid unexpected outages. The system provides a user-friendly dashboard accessible via mobile or web, giving individuals and businesses full visibility into their usage patterns.

What makes this project truly impactful is its scalability and adaptability. It can be integrated into smart homes, commercial kitchens, and even gas distribution networks—offering valuable insights that can drive demand forecasting, inventory planning, and safer distribution practices.

At its core, this project is a response to a real problem with a tech-forward solution. We're not just monitoring gas, we're empowering people with convenience and peace of mind.
